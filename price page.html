Common Questions



Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

Nope. Podcastee’s services are month-to-month with no contract to sign. You can move to a different plan, cancel, or if you choose to move to a different host at anytime, we can help you do that.


Can I pay for a whole year?

Podcastee isn’t set up to support annual payments. We’re strictly month-to-month.


What if I need help?

We’re here to support you and your podcast. If you have questions or need help, we have a complete help section and more importantly, we’re only a quick email here
