    How Do I Find Podcasts to Listen To?

    Finding new podcasts is simple once you get the hang of it.There are a few places you can search to find podcasts you enjoy. Search engines, podcast hosting sites, and social media are a few of the best places.

  1. Google Podcasts by Niche

    2. The quickest way to find a podcast you will enjoy is to Google what you like. Doing an internet search is particularly useful if you have a specific niche you are interested in. For example, you could search for news, film, urban legends, or local history podcasts and see thousands of results.

  2. Check Podcast Hosting Sites

    3. Podcast hosting sites are another easy place to find fun podcasts to listen to. Spotify, Apple, and Amazon are some of the biggest names that offer podcasts through their platforms. When you want to find something new to listen to, type in what you are looking for or browse through the categories until you find something that catches your eye.

  3. Ask Social Media

    4. Social media is an excellent place to crowdsource podcast recommendations in your favorite niche.

    With so many like-minded people online, you should not have any trouble finding something you enjoy.

    Facebook groups, Instagram hashtags, and your regular feed are all accessible places to look for suggestions.
