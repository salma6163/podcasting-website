10 Benefits of Listening to Podcasts for Mental Health



Have you found yourself wondering if you should begin listening to podcasts for your mental health? I think a lot of us have been wondering the same. Ever since podcasts have taken off as one of the top forms of entertainment in recent years, people have been taking to listening to them for mood boosts among many other things. But the question remains, what are the benefits of listening to podcasts?


Disclosure: I am not a medical doctor of any sort. I am simply someone who has experienced the mental health benefits of listening to podcasts. Everything in this post is based on personal experience and is not medical advice.



Why Should People Listen to Podcasts?

In the fast-paced digital age we live in, podcasts have emerged as a captivating and convenient medium for people to stay informed, entertained, and connected. With their ability to cover a wide range of topics, from current events and the latest news to exploring new ideas and personal stories, podcasts have become an integral part of many people’s daily lives.

Are Podcasts Good for Mental Health?


Short answer, yes there are benefits to adding a new podcast to your routine, but there is much more to it. Podcasts can be good for mental health for a variety of reasons. With stress and anxiety levels on the rise, finding effective ways to prioritize mental health has become a popular topic for many.


Podcasts have emerged as a great way to address this need, with their ability to positively impact mental well-being. The popularity of podcasts has led to the findings of many great benefits of listening to podcasts.


Listening to podcasts can help individuals escape from daily stressors and find moments of relaxation and enjoyment, thus offering a positive impact on their mental well-being while offering great background noise for life

Podcasts Can Help You Feel Less Alone

Podcasts have a wonderful ability to help people feel less alone by providing a sense of connection and understanding. Through storytelling, narrative stories, and discussions on various topics, podcasts create a virtual community where people can relate to shared experiences and emotions.


By hearing others’ stories and perspectives, people realize that they are not alone in their struggles, challenges, or unique circumstances, all while going through their normal daily activities, and for me, that is the best medicine.


Whether it’s through podcasts that explore mental health, personal journeys, or societal issues, the power of shared stories, and the comforting presence of a podcast host and their guests, create a space where listeners can find companionship, support, and a reminder that they are part of a larger community.

Where to Listen to Podcasts


There are numerous online platforms where people can listen to podcasts, all of which can be done via your car, headphones during a trip to the grocery store, or from your phone’s speaker while hanging out at home. The luxury of podcasts is that you can listen to them anywhere! Some popular options include:


  1. Spotify

    2. Spotify offers a vast library of podcasts, ranging from popular shows to niche topics. Users can access podcasts for free with ads or opt for a premium subscription to enjoy an ad-free listening experience.

  2. Apple Podcasts:

    3. Apple Podcasts is the default podcast app on Apple devices, making it easily accessible for iPhone, iPad, and Mac users. It provides a comprehensive collection of podcasts across various genres and allows users to subscribe, download, and stream episodes.

  3. Google Podcasts:

    4. Google Podcasts is a platform available for both Android and iOS users. It integrates with Google Assistant, making it convenient to discover and listen to podcasts using voice commands. It also offers personalized recommendations based on listening habits.

  4. Stitcher:

    5. Stitcher is a popular podcast platform known for its user-friendly interface and curated content. It features a diverse range of shows and provides a seamless listening experience across devices.

  5. Overcast:

    6. Overcast is a podcast app for iOS users that offers a clean and intuitive interface. It provides features like Smart Speed, which shortens silences in episodes, and Voice Boost, which enhances audio quality for a better listening experience.
